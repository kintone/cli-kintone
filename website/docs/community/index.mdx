## Support

You can contact our [API Support Desk](https://jp.cybozu.help/k/ja/trouble_shooting/developer/developer_qa.html) for questions about specifications and troubleshooting.

## Feature requests

We accept feature requests on [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/kintone/cli-kintone/issues/new/choose). Your feedback makes cli-kintone better.
